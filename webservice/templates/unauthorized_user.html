If your account is not authorized to use POMS, to get access, do the following:

  • Open the webpage to SNOW and login.
  • Select the tile "Request Something".
  • Select the tile "Experiment/Project/Collaboration Computing Account".
  • Choose your experiment.
  • Choose the role "Production" or "Analysis" (production shifters require "analysis" role only).
  • Production shifters only: request your experiment's production team to mark you as a shifter in POMS.
Please do not fill out an incident or request to POMS as access must be granted by your experiment.
